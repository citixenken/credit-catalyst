## The Problem We Solve

Across East Africa, **47 million SMEs** struggle to access formal credit. The few who do face a broken system:

- **Rigid repayments** that ignore cash flow — a merchant earning KES 1.7M/month defaults on a KES 69K payment because it landed on the wrong week
- **No transparency** — "Application denied" with no explanation and no path forward
- **Reactive collections** — banks discover defaults after the damage is done, when recovery costs 5–8x more than prevention
- **Siloed channels** — M-Pesa data, POS data, and bank data never talk to each other

The result: **8–12% default rates**, low uptake, and merchants who churn to informal lenders.

---

## What Credit Catalyst Does

### 1. Repayments That Flex With the Business
Instead of fixed monthly amounts, repayments automatically adjust to real cash flow. Slow week? Lower payment. Strong week? Higher payment. **Same total repaid — zero missed payments.**

> *Real example: James Kariuki's February repayment dropped from KES 68,750 to KES 45,000 when revenue dipped. March normalised. No default. No phone call. No stress.*

### 2. Defaults Prevented, Not Collected
The system monitors merchant health continuously. When it detects early signs of distress — revenue dropping, zero-balance days — it **proactively restructures** the loan before the merchant misses a payment.

**This is not debt forgiveness. It's intelligent rescheduling that protects both the bank and the merchant.**

### 3. A Credit Score Merchants Want to Improve
Every score change comes with a plain-English reason. Merchants see exactly what affects their score and what to do next. A clear tier system (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond) unlocks real rewards at each level — higher limits, lower rates, partner discounts.

**The score becomes a growth tool, not a judgement.**

### 4. One System, Every Channel
Web, mobile, partner portals, USSD, WhatsApp — one intelligence layer powers them all. New channels launch in **weeks, not months**.
