## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM (Market Pain)

### The $4.7B Merchant Lending Gap in East Africa

**Four systemic failures plague merchant credit today:**

| # | Problem | Real-World Impact |
|---|---------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Rigid repayment schedules** | Fixed monthly amounts ignore that merchant cash flow fluctuates 30–60% week to week |
| 2 | **Fragmented credit systems** | A merchant's M-Pesa data, POS data, and bank data live in silos — no single view of creditworthiness |
| 3 | **Black-box decisions** | "Application denied" with zero explanation — merchants have no path to improve |
| 4 | **Reactive default management** | Banks detect defaults *after* they happen, not before — recovery costs 5–8x more than prevention |

### Why This Matters Now

- **47M+ SMEs** across Sub-Saharan Africa are underserved by formal credit
- Merchant lending NPL rates average **8–12%** across the region
- Digital transaction data is exploding — but **fewer than 5%** of lenders use it for real-time decisioning
- Post-COVID, merchants demand flexibility as a baseline, not a premium

**The market is ready. The data exists. What's missing is the intelligence layer.**

## SLIDE 3 — THE SOLUTION

### Credit Catalyst: Always-On, AI-Adaptive Merchant Credit

Credit Catalyst is not another lending app. It is an **orchestration layer** that sits between the bank's existing systems and the merchant — making credit intelligent, responsive, and self-correcting.

**Three breakthrough capabilities:**

### 1. AI Flex-Pay Engine
Repayments that breathe with the business.

- Ingests real-time transaction data via the **Pulse API** (M-Pesa, POS, bank, e-commerce)
- Predicts cash flow 7–14 days ahead
- Automatically adjusts the next repayment amount — lower during slow weeks, higher during strong ones
- **Same total repaid. Zero missed payments. Zero stress.**

> *Live demo: James Kariuki's February repayment was auto-adjusted from KES 68,750 down to KES 45,000 when AI detected a revenue dip — then normalized in March. No default. No intervention. No phone call.*

### 2. Self-Healing Loans
Prevent defaults before they happen.

- AI monitors 12+ risk signals continuously
- When it predicts distress (revenue drop >30%, zero-balance days), it **proactively restructures** — extending term, pausing repayment, or adjusting rate
- The merchant stays current. The bank avoids write-offs.
- **This is not debt forgiveness — it's intelligent rescheduling that protects both parties.**

### 3. Transparent AI Scoring
A credit score merchants can actually improve.

- 5-factor model: Payment History (35%), Revenue Consistency (25%), Credit Utilisation (20%), Business Age (10%), Industry Risk (10%)
- Every score change comes with a **plain-English reason**: *"Your score went up 4 points because of consistent revenue for 7 consecutive days"*
- Merchants see a clear **milestone path**: Bronze (650) → Silver (700) → Gold (742) → Platinum (800) → Diamond (850)
- Each tier unlocks tangible rewards: higher limits, lower rates, partner discounts

> *This turns a credit score from a judgement into a game the merchant wants to win.*
